Menopause

Menopause is a gradual transition in the female body; ovarian production of female hormones decreases, menstrual cycles become irregular and eventually stop altogether. Unpleasant symptoms can include night sweats, hot flashes, nervousness, sleep disturbances, hair loss, skin changes, or loss of libido.
In addition to a detailed medical history and gynecological examination, blood hormone analysis may sometimes be necessary to make an individualized diagnosis. We will gladly discuss the findings with you and advise you on the advantages and disadvantages of hormone replacement therapy to find the best possible treatment for you.
Hormone replacement therapy can quickly improve menopausal symptoms, but we can also advise you on treatment alternatives such as homeopathic remedies or soy and red clover preparations. We are also happy to discuss herbal approaches.
